# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Work Hours and Remote Work Policy
- **Standard Work Hours**: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time, Monday through Friday.
- **Core Collaboration Hours**: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.
- **Remote Work Policy**: Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week with approval from their manager. A minimum of 2 days per week must be spent in the local office.

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO) and Holidays
- **Annual Vacation Leave**: Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per calendar year, accrued monthly at 1.67 days per month.
- **Sick Leave**: Full-time employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. Sick leaves exceeding 2 consecutive days require a medical certificate/doctor's note to be submitted to HR.
- **Company Holidays**: The company observes 10 standard federal/national holidays, which are listed on the HR intranet calendar.
- **Parental Leave**: 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave is available for new parents (birth, adoption, or foster placement) after 1 year of continuous service.

## 3. Benefits and Health Insurance
- **Health Insurance**: Medical, dental, and vision coverage begins on the first day of the month following the employee's start date. The company covers 80% of the employee's premium.
- **Retirement (401k)**: The company matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of their annual salary. Vesting of the company match is immediate.
- **Wellness Stipend**: Employees are eligible for a wellness reimbursement of up to $50 per month for gym memberships, fitness classes, or health apps.

## 4. Timesheet Submission and Guidelines
- **Timesheet Deadline**: Timesheets must be updated and submitted for approval by 5:00 PM every Friday.
- **Project Billing**: Hours must be logged to the specific client project codes. Administrative or internal work should be logged under the "INTERNAL" project code.
- **Unlocking Timesheets**: If a timesheet is submitted in error, contact your manager or use the self-service portal to request an unlock before the payroll processing window closes on Monday morning at 10:00 AM.
